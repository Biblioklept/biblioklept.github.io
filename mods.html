- Ammo Crafting Smoothbrainified Ultimate Edition TTW - for Tale of Two Wastelands.

simple mod that makes the system for ammo crafting much simpler, where as before there were various types of casings, powders, and primers, they are now just simply shellcasings, powders, and primers. this also adds recipes that weren't there before, and the ability to turn surplus ammo into ingredients as well as surplus energy ammo into normal.
