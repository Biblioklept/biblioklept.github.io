### Browsers
- [Blink (Chrome)](https://helium.computer/)

Lightweight and fast – minimal interface, optimized for speed.

Privacy-friendly defaults – blocks trackers and ads automatically.

Simple, distraction-free browsing – focuses on content, not clutter.

- [Gecko (Firefox)](https://librewolf.net/)

Privacy-first Firefox fork – removes telemetry and tracking.

Enhanced security – hardened settings and no data collection.

Customizable and open-source – fully transparent and tweakable.

Either of these work, and are great forks of their respective engines that come with privacy-respecting tweaks without harming everyday usage.

### VPNs
- [Mullvad](https://mullvad.net/)

No personal info required – account is just a random number, nothing to track.

Proven privacy – audited, no logs, can’t be forced to hand over data.

Strong, honest security – open-source, modern protocols, no gimmicks.

Can have issues with torrenting. Try using **Local network sharing** in **VPN settings** for a fix.
